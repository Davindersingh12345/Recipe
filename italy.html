Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:
350g spaghetti
150g guanciale or pancetta, diced
3 large eggs
75g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
50g Parmesan cheese, grated
Freshly ground black pepper
Salt

Recipe:

Cook spaghetti until al dente, then drain.
Cook diced guanciale or pancetta until crispy.
Whisk together eggs and grated cheeses.
Toss hot spaghetti with cooked guanciale.
Pour egg mixture over spaghetti, tossing to coat.
Add reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.
Season with black pepper.
Serve immediately.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients:
Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
Tomato sauce
Fresh mozzarella cheese
Fresh basil leaves
Extra virgin olive oil
Salt and black pepper

Recipe:

Preheat oven to 500°F (260°C).
Roll out pizza dough into 12-inch circles.
Spread tomato sauce over dough.
Arrange mozzarella slices and basil leaves on top.
Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
Slice and serve hot.

Osso Buco alla Milanese

Ingredients:
Veal shanks
Flour for dredging
Salt and pepper
Butter
Olive oil
Onion, garlic, carrot, celery
Dry white wine
Diced tomatoes
Beef or veal broth
Tomato paste
Dried thyme and oregano
Bay leaf
Optional: lemon zest
Gremolata (for garnish)

Recipe:

Season and dredge veal shanks, then brown in butter and olive oil.
Sauté onion, garlic, carrot, and celery.
Deglaze with white wine, then add tomatoes, broth, tomato paste, herbs, and lemon zest.
Return veal to the pot, cover, and braise in the oven at 350°F (175°C) for 2-2.5 hours.
Serve hot, garnished with gremolata.
