Mexican Cuisine

Tacos Al Pastor

Ingredients:

2 dried guajillo chilies
2 dried ancho chilies
Garlic, onion
Pineapple juice
White vinegar
Achiote paste
Spices (oregano, cumin, paprika, cinnamon)
Salt
Pork shoulder
Corn tortillas
Pineapple slices
Cilantro
White onion
Lime wedges

Recipe:

Soak and blend chilies with garlic, onion, pineapple juice, vinegar, achiote paste, spices, and salt.
Marinate thinly sliced pork in the mixture for at least 2 hours.
Grill pork slices until charred and cooked through.
Warm corn tortillas and fill with grilled pork, pineapple slices, cilantro, and diced onion.
Serve with lime wedges.

Mole Poblano

Ingredients:

Dried ancho, guajillo, and pasilla chilies
Sesame seeds, almonds, raisins, pumpkin seeds
Vegetable oil
Onion, garlic
Masa harina
Ground cloves, cinnamon, cumin, oregano
Chicken broth
Mexican chocolate
Salt, sugar (optional)

Recipe:

Toast chilies, seeds, nuts, and raisins.
Sauté onion and garlic, then blend with toasted ingredients, masa harina, spices, and chicken broth until smooth.
Simmer with Mexican chocolate until thickened. Adjust seasoning and consistency.

For Chicken:
Chicken thighs
Salt, pepper
Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions for Chicken:
Season chicken thighs and fry until golden and cooked through.

Serve Mole Poblano:
Spoon sauce over cooked rice and crispy chicken thighs.
Garnish with sesame seeds and cilantro.

Chiles Rellenos

Ingredients:

4 large poblano peppers
Vegetable oil
1/2 onion, minced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup cooked and shredded chicken or beef (optional)
1 cup cooked rice
1 cup shredded cheese
Salt and pepper
3 large eggs, separated
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
Tomato sauce or salsa
Crema or yogurt
Fresh cilantro leaves

Recipe:

Char poblano peppers, remove skin, seeds, and membranes.
Sauté onion and garlic, add meat (if using), rice, cheese, salt, and pepper for filling.
Stuff peppers with filling mixture.
Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
Mix egg yolks, flour, baking powder, and salt.
Fold egg whites into yolk mixture.
Dip stuffed peppers in batter, fry until golden brown.
Serve hot with tomato sauce, crema or yogurt, and cilantro.
