Palak Paneer

Ingredients:
  • 500g fresh spinach leaves
  • 250g paneer (Indian cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric, coriander, garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
Recipe:

Blanch spinach leaves, then blend with onion, garlic, ginger, and green chilies to make a puree. Fry paneer cubes until golden brown. Set aside.

In a pot, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Sauté chopped onions until translucent. Add chopped tomatoes, cook until mushy, then add spices (turmeric, coriander, garam masala, red chili powder, salt).

Pour in spinach puree, simmer for 5-7 minutes. Add fried paneer cubes, mix gently, and simmer for a few more minutes. Adjust seasoning if needed.

Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice.

Dal Makhni

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup whole black lentils (urad dal)
  • 1/4 cup kidney beans (rajma)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit lengthwise
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp red chili powder, turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/4 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
Recipe:

Soak lentils and beans for 6 hours or overnight, then pressure cook with salt until soft. In a pan, heat ghee and oil. Add cumin seeds, onions, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and sauté till golden.

Add tomatoes, cook till soft. Add spices and cook for a minute. Add cooked lentils and beans with water. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.

Mash some lentils, add cream, and simmer for another 5-10 minutes. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.

Biryani

Ingredients:
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices for marination (ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala)
  • Salt
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Whole spices (bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 2 large onions
  • Green chilies
  • Ghee or oil
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Cashews and raisins for garnish (optional)
Recipe:

Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and herbs. Cook soaked rice with whole spices until 70-80% done. Fry onions until golden brown.

Cook marinated chicken with onions and chilies. Layer partially cooked rice over chicken, add remaining fried onions, herbs, and saffron.

Cook covered on low heat for 20-25 minutes. Garnish with fried cashews and raisins. Serve hot with raita or salan.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • For filling: 3 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Fresh coriander leaves
Recipe:

Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek overnight, then grind into a smooth batter. Let it ferment for 12-24 hours.

For the filling, heat oil, add mustard seeds, then onions and green chilies. Add turmeric, mashed potatoes, and fresh coriander. Cook briefly.

Heat a dosa pan, pour a ladle of batter, spread it thinly, cook until crispy, then add the potato filling and fold. Serve hot with chutney.
